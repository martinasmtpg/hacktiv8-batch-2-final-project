MERCURE HOTEL

Tentang Mercure Hotel

Description

Located at Galuh Mas complex in the heart of Karawang City, Mercure Karawang is within walking distance to Karawang Central Plaza. The hotel is also only 10 minutes by car to the industrial estates and 5 minutes to city hall of Karawang.

Karawang is located approximately 30 miles or 60 km east of Jakarta. It is a new prime industrial area with the various large international industrial estates and heavy industries. The city is home to many Japanese industrial giants.
